Our Programs

Full Time Hifz
  • Our main and longest running program
  • Classes occur Monday-Friday
  • Timings: Monday-Thursday = 8:15am-3:15pm, Friday's = 8:15am-12:30pm
  • Breaks are given for Snacks, Lunch, and Zuhur Salah
  • Students are provided a positive learning environment throughout the day
  • Subjects include(in addition to hifz): Fiqh, Akhlaaq, Tajweed, Seerah
  • Students are taught in a practical manner to ensure application of knowledge
Part-Time Hifz
  • Program constructed for students that wish to memorize the Glorious Qur'aan while taking care of other responsibilities
  • Classes occur Monday-Thursday
  • Timings: Monday-Thursday = 5:15pm-6:45pm
Summer Hifz
  • Program constructed for students that wish to memorize the Glorious Qur'aan during the summer holidays
  • Program begins from July and concludes end of August(Ramadhan may change schedule)
  • Classes occur Monday-Friday
  • Timings: Monday-Thursday = 8:15am-3:15pm, Friday's = 8:15am-12:30pm
  • Breaks are given for Snacks, Lunch, and Zuhur Salah
  • Students are provided a positive learning environment throughout the day
  • Subjects include(in addition to hifz): Fiqh, Akhlaaq, Tajweed, Seerah
  • Students are taught in a practical manner to ensure application of knowledge
  • Students are also scheduled a Field Trip!
